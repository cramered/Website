About Me

I have since made great use of such education. I've put to use my knowledge in Psychology in Stress Coaching at Boston College. Additionally, I volunteer at a suicide hotline, and have learned many practices there. In Computer Science, I have created projects that I am quite proud of and have been given the opportunity to work at some great companies.
